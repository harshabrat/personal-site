AmbedOntoS: Large Scale Ontology Synthesis for Ambedkar and Peace Studies

A published research work on large-scale ontology synthesis for Ambedkar and Peace Studies, presented in the International Conference on Data Science, Computation and Security.

November 14, 2025
◦
  • publications

Authors:

Anubrat Bora, Gerard Deepak, Harshabrat Bora

Publication details:

  • Book: International Conference on Data Science, Computation and Security
  • Pages: 183-193
  • Publisher: Springer Nature Switzerland
  • Publication date: 2025/11/14

This paper presents a strategic framework for ontology synthesis dedicated to Ambedkar and Peace Studies as a domain of choice. The proposed system extracts terms and categories from domain-specific datasets to establish a foundational semantic structure, then uses metadata generation, classification, and language model based knowledge expansion to support large-scale ontology development.

Key areas:

  • Ontology synthesis for specialized socioeconomic and societal domains.
  • Metadata generation using OpenCalais.
  • Classification through DistilBERT for automatic feature extraction and contextual classification.
  • Knowledge generation with Latent Semantic Indexing and large language models such as Llama-4-Scout and Mistral-Medium-3.
