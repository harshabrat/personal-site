About

 

Hi, I am Harshabrat, an aspiring data scientist with a growing interest in turning messy real-world data into clear, useful insights.

I write about the tools, techniques, and small experiments I pick up along the way: Python, statistics, machine learning basics, and the occasional side project. This site is mostly a notebook for things I want to remember and share.

When I am not exploring datasets, I am usually reading, tinkering with code, or looking for the next problem worth visualizing.

Feel free to reach out via the links in the footer. I am always happy to connect with fellow learners and builders.
